Meet Dr.Slava Fuzayloff

Dr. Fuzayloff is a competent, energetic and highly dedicated physician who is board certified in Internal Medicine. He is a Diplomat of the American Board of Internal Medicine and has built a highly respected private practice in New York City. His extensive schooling includes a degree in Biology from St. John's University in New York and a medical degree from New York College of Osteopathic Medicine, where he graduated with honors in Pathology and Nephrology.


After medical school, Dr. Fuzayloff completed his residency in Internal Medicine at New York Hospital of Queens. His time there has included extensive training at Memorial Sloane-Kettering Hospitalâ€”one of the world's premier cancer care facilities.


Dr. Fuzayloff is affiliated with Lenox Hill Hospital and works closely with other hospital in New York City, including New York Presbyterian (Columbia and Cornell), NYU, and Saint Luke's-Roosevelt. His professional interests include sexually transmitted disease and general internal medicine.


As an active member of the American Medical association, the New York State Medical Association and the American Osteopathic Association, Dr. Fuzayloff is able to stay abreast of the latest advancements and medical treatments. His dedication to his patients' well-being has earned him a reputation as a doctor who is not only knowledgeable, but one whose bedside manner proves his commitment to his patients' health and happiness.


Although medicine is his first love, the doctor enjoys unwinding with long, relaxing hikes through New York's abundant and beautiful woodlands and the occasional exhilarating game of ice hockey. Doctor Fuzayloff has been known to wield a hockey stick as well as he does a stethoscope.
