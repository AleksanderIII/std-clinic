All you need to know about Gonorrhea

Gonorrhea (the clap) is a widespread, sexually transmitted bacterial infection. Each year over 500,000 gonorrhea cases are reported in the United States. Around half of these cases are diagnosed among individuals aged 15-24 years old.

It can affect genital, oral, and anal areas and causes serious reproductive health problems if left untreated. It is important to recognize the symptoms and signs of this curable condition and get a proper one-time treatment for gonorrhea.
