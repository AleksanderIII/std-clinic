Gonorrhea testing algorithms

Here we summarize the gonorrhea testing for different common situations:

  • Screening for females
  • Screening for males
  • Testing after potential exposure with no symptoms
  • Testing after potential exposure with symptoms

Screening is when testing is done with no particular exposure in mind and with no symptoms—just as a routine test.


Let’s go over those algorithms and see which one is best for your particular situation.

Gonorrhea Screening Recommendations for Asymptomatic Females

This screening algorithm is for females with no symptoms and no concern about one particular exposure.

Gonorrhea Screening Recommendations for Asymptomatic Males

This screening algorithm is for females with no symptoms and no concern about one particular exposure.

Post-Exposure Gonorrhea Testing Algorithm: With and without Symptoms

This algorithm is for people who have had recent exposure to a gonorrhea-positive person or have current gonorrhea symptoms.


*Please pay attention to the fact that symptomatic people can get tested at any time.
