Who needs to get tested for herpes?

There are two main reasons for someone to undergo herpes testing:

  • Medical reason

There are some signs and symptoms and a diagnosis need to be established.

It is done mainly when someone has skin lesions to rule out other skin conditions that look similar to herpes.

  • Personal reason

A patient wants to know their herpes status after exposure to a herpes-positive partner or just to know their baseline herpes status.
