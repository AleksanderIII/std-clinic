IF YOU HAVE HEALTH INSURANCE THAT WE
 DON’T ACCEPT

We accept some but not all health insurance plans. If you have health insurance that we don’t accept, your office visit will cost $90 if you pay with with a credit card or $84 if you pay cash.

Fees for all necessary tests will be covered by your health insurance (even if we don’t accept it for office visit charges) becasue we have contracts with big national labs (Quest, Northwell, Labcorp) and will send your tests to a lab that is ”in-network” for your health insurance.

People Choose STDCenterNY for Multiple Reasons:

Same day treatment

We provide same-day treatment, if necessary, to give you peace of mind while waiting for the test results.

Private, accurate testing

All testing that we do is 100% private and confidential. We ask that you call us three days after your visit to get your results. We can send you a report showing the results by email (but we’ll do that only if you ask us to).


We use several CLIA-certified labs (Quest, Northwell, LabCorp, etc.) that accept your insurance (in network) and guarantee the accuracy of your results.

Convenient location

Our office is conveniently located in Midtown Manhattan, near Grand Central Station. We offer same-day appointments, including evening and weekend hours.

All patients seen by a doctor

All our patients are seen by our board-certified STD doctor, not a PA or NP as in our competitors’ clinics. Doctors get better and longer education and training in all areas of medicine. It makes a big difference, since STD testing needs to be done at a specific time after your potential exposure, the correct version of the test needs to be ordered, and preventative treatment can be initiated while awaiting test results.

Most insurance accepted. Discount for uninsured

At STDCenterNY, we accept most medical insurance. Insurance usually covers both preventative services (screening) and treatment. Usually, there’s no limit to the number of times you can have STD testing in a year. If you’re in doubt, please call your health insurance provider to ask about that. * Please provide your insurance company with the following codes: “CPT 99204” and “ICD Z 11.13” - for screening“ “CPT 99204” and “ICD N 34.1” if you are symptomatic. If you’re not insured, we offer affordable self-pay rates, which you can see on this page. We value our reputation, so we don’t upsell. Before doing any tests, the doctor will talk to you and explain the ideal way and the economical way to deal with your situation
